Design Principles

Kelton J. Green

Contrast

  • Chess.com - Play Chess Online

The contrast between the bright green squares of the chess board and the play button are in great contrast to the dark background and the white/grey text that cover the information on the site.

Rule of Thirds

  • AlternativeTo - Crowdsourced software reccomendations

I think the header of the site at the top of the page, the information of how much stuff and people interact on AlternativeTo, and the articles on the bottom of the page create a great example of the Rule of Thirds when you initially click on the site.

White Space and Clean Design

  • PCPartPicker - Pick parts. Build your PC.

The nice spacing of text, icons, buttons and example builds with pictures creates a lot of pleasant space and keeps the site looking clean.
