Target Audience

Target audience for this site is people who are older than 12 years of age living in the cities around city of Twin Falls. They look for local events and activities in and around Twin Falls. They try to attend events or league games that are affordable with friends and families to enjoy.

persona 1

Bob Builder

A middle age man who has a couple sons and daughters who attend the local public schools. He is a trained plumber who owns his own business, so he can mostly choose his own hours. His wife also works, but from home, as a graphic designer. He is constantly looking for ways to spend time with his kids and wife, while being able to maintain his work life.

persona 2

Jackie Chan

Jackie owns her own restaurant and offers catering to local events, she tries to stay on top of local events so that she can hopefully provide catering to them and promote her business, while making money and making sure that there isn't any bad weather when she does so.

Five Scenarios

  • Does this site provide information about power outages in the area?
  • Can I provide community communication through a forum on the site?
  • Are youth programs promoted?
  • Local High Schools news on sports and extra-curricular?
  • Is there an hourly display of how the weather will be?
